Aqua Entrepreneurs Development Program for Livelihood (AQUAEDP)

Project Details

The Aqua Entrepreneurs Development Program for Livelihood (AquaEDP) initiative advocates for the advancement of livelihoods and entrepreneurship in aquaculture. It focuses on promoting sustainable economic activities in the tribal districts of the Vidarbha region, contributing to economic development and food security.

The Aqua Entrepreneurs Development Program for Livelihood (AquaEDP) initiative actively advocates for the advancement of livelihoods and entrepreneurship in aquaculture, aiming to secure investments from governmental employment generation programs. The primary focus is on promoting sustainable economic activities in the tribal districts of the Vidarbha region, ultimately contributing to both economic development and ensuring food security in the area.

Objectives
  • To create aqua entrepreneurs across the value chain.
  • To enhance productivity and annual income of 5000 farmers.
  • To develop and promote six Fish Farmer Producer Organizations (FFPOs) through PMMSY and the State Departments.
  • To include aquaculture into local NGOs' working in the livelihood domain for expanding the coverage of aquatic livelihood.
